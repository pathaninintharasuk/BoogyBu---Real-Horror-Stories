About BoogyBu

Welcome to BoogyBu, a collection of the most spine-chilling, real horror stories from around the world. Here, ordinary people share their extraordinary encounters with the supernatural, paranormal, and unexplained.

Whether you've witnessed a ghost, experienced something paranormal, or lived through a real-life horror story, this is the place to share your tale. Every story is treated with respect and care, creating a community where people can share their most frightening experiences.

Guidelines for Submission

  • Stories should be based on real experiences or genuine folklore
  • Keep content appropriate - scary but not gratuitously violent
  • Respect others' beliefs and experiences
  • Longer, detailed stories are preferred
  • Include location if relevant to the story
